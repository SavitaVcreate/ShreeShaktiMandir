Who is Maa Durga

Maa Durga is one of the most powerful and respected goddesses in Hinduism. She represents divine power, protection, strength, courage, and motherhood. Maa Durga is worshipped as the protector of good and the destroyer of evil.

She is commonly shown riding a lion or tiger and carrying divine weapons in her many hands. Maa Durga symbolizes fearlessness, positivity, and victory over negative forces. Devotees worship her during Navratri and Durga Puja festivals with great devotion and faith.

Maa Durga inspires people to remain strong during difficult times and teaches the importance of truth, righteousness, and inner strength.
